About

I am an avid consumer of anything related to video games and digital art.

I am currently in my second year of GIMM (Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile) from Boise State University where I have gained experience in game development with unity, 3D modeling, and web development.

I have been doing digital art for six years and ended up participating in artfight for the last two years. It is a skill that I have invested the most time into compared to the rest of my skills. It is something I am able to give people some direction and feedback on, even when I'm the one desiring it the most from others.

My artist endeavors led me to doing graphic design for my previous workplace where I used figma for everything. I learned about figma through my major and needing to rework an application's UI. I reworked the UI of mobile Spotify and recreating it sure had my work cut out for me but I got it done looking just like the original.
